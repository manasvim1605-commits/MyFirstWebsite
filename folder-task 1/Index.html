About Cold Coffee

Cold coffee is a smooth and refreshing drink made with coffee, milk, sugar, and ice. Itâ€™s perfect for summer days or when you want something tasty and chilled!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream (optional)

How to Make Cold Coffee

1. Add milk, coffee, sugar, and ice cubes to a blender.

2. Blend until smooth and frothy.

3. Pour into a tall glass.

4. Add ice cream on top for extra taste!

5. Serve chilled and enjoy!
